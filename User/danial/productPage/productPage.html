HUJAN X MYPO

By Petronas Orchestra

Malaysia’s alternative rock band Hujan has enjoyed a cult following throughout the years. Get your tickets to listen an experience of Rock mix with orchestra.

Description

Malaysia’s alternative rock band Hujan has enjoyed a cult following throughout the years.After its smashing DFP debut in 2013, Hujan is back with its first-everperformance with the MPYO, performing popular hits such as “Bila Aku SudahTiada”, “DugaanNya” and “Mencari Konklusi”. Blending hints of alternative rockand jazz melodies, Hujan’s unorthodox rock music reaches dizzying heights whenpaired with the energetic MPYO and the orchestra’s spellbinding symphonicarrangements.
